About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an intense survival action game where you must fend off waves of increasingly difficult monsters. Collect power-ups, upgrade your character, and see how long you can last against the relentless hordes!

With simple controls but deep gameplay mechanics, Monster Survivors offers an addictive gaming experience that will keep you coming back for more. Can you become the ultimate survivor?

Endless Gameplay

Face wave after wave of monsters in an endless survival challenge that gets progressively more difficult.

Character Upgrades

Collect experience and power-ups to upgrade your character's abilities and weapons.

Monster Variety

Battle against dozens of unique monster types, each with their own attack patterns and behaviors.

How to Play

  1. Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character around the map.
  2. Automatically attack nearby monsters with your equipped weapon.
  3. Collect experience gems from defeated monsters to level up.
  4. Choose new abilities or upgrade existing ones when you level up.
  5. Survive as long as possible and aim for a high score!
